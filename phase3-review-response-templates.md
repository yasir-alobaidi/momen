# Mo'men Store — Review Response Templates (Phase 3.4)

**For:** Professional owner responses to Google reviews, ready to adapt once real customer reviews are in.
**Note:** the reviews themselves still have to be genuine — Google prohibits incentivized, fake, or self-authored reviews. These templates are only for replying to real customer feedback, not generating it.

---

## Before you reply — quick guidelines

- Reply within a few days if you can — response speed itself signals an active, cared-for business.
- Keep it short. Two to four sentences is plenty; long replies read as defensive.
- Use the reviewer's name if Google shows one.
- Don't argue in public. If a complaint needs real back-and-forth, invite them to a phone number or email in the reply instead of hashing it out in the thread.
- Sign off with a first name or "— Mo'men Store" so it reads as a person, not a bot.

---

## Positive reviews (4–5 stars)

**General thank-you**
> Thank you, [Name]! We're glad you had a good experience at Mo'men Store — hope to see you again soon.

**Mentions specific staff or service**
> Thanks so much, [Name]! We'll make sure [staff name / the team] hears this — it means a lot. See you next time!

**Short or generic ("great store", "good service")**
> Thank you for the kind words, [Name] — really appreciate you taking the time to leave a review!

---

## Mixed reviews (3 stars)

**Good overall, one specific complaint**
> Thanks for the honest feedback, [Name]. Glad you liked [thing they praised] — and we hear you on [issue raised]. We're working on it. Hope to give you a five-star visit next time.

---

## Negative reviews (1–2 stars)

**Slow service / long wait**
> Sorry about the wait, [Name] — that's not the experience we want for anyone stopping by. We're looking at staffing during busy hours to fix this. Thanks for flagging it.

**Item out of stock**
> Sorry we didn't have what you were looking for, [Name]. We're working on keeping shelves better stocked — if there's something specific you need regularly, let us know and we'll try to keep it on hand.

**Pricing complaint**
> We hear you on pricing, [Name] — we try to stay fair and competitive for the neighborhood. Thanks for the feedback.

**Cleanliness / store condition**
> Thank you for letting us know, [Name] — this isn't the standard we hold ourselves to, and we're addressing it right away. Hope you'll give us another chance to make it right.

**Unfriendly staff / service complaint**
> We're sorry to hear this, [Name] — that's not how we want anyone to feel at Mo'men Store. We're addressing it with the team directly. If you're willing to share more, please reach out at [phone/email] — we'd like to make this right.

---

## Neutral / factual reviews

> Thanks for stopping by and sharing your thoughts, [Name] — we appreciate it and hope to see you again.
