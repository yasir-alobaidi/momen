## Before you start
- Attributes available depend on your primary category (**Convenience Store**) and country (**Jordan**) — not every item below is guaranteed to actually appear as an option when you get to the dashboard. Treat this as "things to check for," not a guarantee every box exists.
- Only mark something "yes" if it's genuinely and continuously true. Google can quietly strip an attribute later if enough customer reviews contradict it.
- Edits are reviewed before going live — usually within about 10 minutes, but Google says it can occasionally take up to 30 days.
