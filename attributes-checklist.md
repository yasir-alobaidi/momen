# Google Business Profile — Attributes Checklist (Phase 3.2)

> Supports README.md Phase 3.2: "Add missing profile attributes (accessibility, parking, payment methods, Wi-Fi)." This isn't something that can be added to the live GBP dashboard from here — it's a walkthrough to fill in yourself (or with whoever's on-site) *before* you sit down at the dashboard, so you're checking boxes from a decision you've already made instead of guessing in the moment.

## Before you start
- Attributes available depend on your primary category (**Convenience Store**) and country (**Jordan**) — not every item below is guaranteed to actually appear as an option when you get to the dashboard. Treat this as "things to check for," not a guarantee every box exists.
- Only mark something "yes" if it's genuinely and continuously true. Google can quietly strip an attribute later if enough customer reviews contradict it.
- Edits are reviewed before going live — usually within about 10 minutes, but Google says it can occasionally take up to 30 days.

## Accessibility
- [ ] Wheelchair accessible entrance
- [ ] Wheelchair accessible parking (only relevant if there's a dedicated lot/spot — see Parking below)
- [ ] Wheelchair accessible restroom (skip if there's no public restroom at all)

## Parking
- [ ] Free parking lot on-site
- [ ] Free street parking nearby
- [ ] Paid parking nearby
- [ ] No dedicated parking (street-front only) — if that's the reality, it's fine to leave these unchecked rather than force one

## Payment methods
> Heads up: some regions/categories have had payment attributes removed from the dashboard entirely — Google's reasoning was that most businesses already take most modern payment types, so the filter added little search value. If this section doesn't show up at all once you're in the dashboard, that's expected, not something broken.
- [ ] Credit cards
- [ ] Debit cards
- [ ] NFC mobile payment (Apple Pay / Google Pay tap-to-pay)
- [ ] Cash only / cash accepted

## Wi-Fi
- [ ] Free Wi-Fi for customers
- [ ] No Wi-Fi available

## Service options (bonus — not named in 3.2, but likely relevant for a convenience store)
- [ ] In-store shopping
- [ ] In-store pickup (only if phone/WhatsApp orders for pickup are actually a thing)
- [ ] Delivery (only if delivery is a standing service — the site lists a WhatsApp contact, but that's for general inquiries, not necessarily an order/delivery line; confirm before checking this)

## When you're actually at the dashboard
1. Edit profile → look for an "Attributes" or "Info" section
2. Go family by family (Accessibility, Amenities/Wi-Fi, Payments, Service options) and toggle Yes/No to match what's checked above
3. Save, then give it up to a day before confirming it shows correctly on the live listing
