Overview

Mo'men Store ("we," "our," or "the Store") operates this website to share information about our convenience store located in Amman, Jordan. This Privacy Policy explains what information this site interacts with, how it is handled, and your rights regarding it. This is an informational website only — it does not currently include an online store, customer accounts, or a contact form.

Information We Collect

This website does not ask you to sign up, log in, or submit a form, so we do not directly collect personal information such as your name or email address through the site itself.

Like virtually every website, our hosting provider automatically records basic technical information when you visit — such as IP address, browser type, device type, and the pages viewed. This is standard server activity logging used for security and performance, not for identifying individual visitors.

If we add features in the future, such as an order form, newsletter sign-up, or analytics tool, we will update this policy beforehand to describe exactly what is collected and why.

Cookies

This website does not currently set cookies or use third-party advertising or tracking scripts. If that changes — for example, if we add Google Analytics — this section will be updated, and where required by law we will request your consent first.

Links to Other Sites

Our site links out to Google Maps so you can find directions to our store, and may include a phone or WhatsApp link so you can contact us directly. When you follow these links, you leave our website and become subject to that service's own privacy policy. We do not control, and are not responsible for, how those third parties handle your information.

How Information Is Used

Any information automatically logged through normal website hosting is used only to operate, secure, and maintain this website — for example, to detect unusual traffic or fix technical issues. We do not sell, rent, or share visitor information with advertisers or other third parties.

Data Retention & Security

We retain standard server logs only as long as necessary for security and operational purposes, after which they are routinely deleted or anonymized. We take reasonable measures to protect this website, but no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Jordan's Personal Data Protection Law (Law No. 24 of 2023) gives individuals rights regarding personal data processed about them, including the right to be informed, to access data held about them, to request correction, and to object to or withdraw consent for processing, where applicable. Because this website does not collect personal data beyond standard technical server logs, these rights are most relevant if you contact us directly by phone, WhatsApp, or in person — in which case any information you share with us is used only for the purpose you contacted us for.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this site.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our website or services change. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. We encourage you to check back occasionally.
